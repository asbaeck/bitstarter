Do you want to learn to speak a new language but do not have any time to sit at a book or computer to learn? Now you can! LeLaWalks is a language learning service that uses only audio: you can download the learning materials as mp3 files and listen to them anytime anywhere.

Change your walking tours, commuting to work or even household work to effective language learning time!

And what's more: you can customise our training material to match exactly with your needs and level. No more wasting time with words and things you already know. LeLaaWalk will be open soon and it will offer German as the first language to learn. French, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese and Kiswahili will follow later.

You start learning a new language with us by going through the Level A material. It includes simples dialogues and vocabulary. Thanks to the flexiblity of our material and system, you can choose the order of topic completely freely, and can skip the topics that seem irrelevant. You can also compile your own versions of the material that include just the things that you want to learn. We'll keep record of which modules you have downloaded and support you in testing your progress and building effective packages for repeating past lessons.

Take a few LeLaWalks and see how easily you can learn to speak and understand a new language, and a whole new world opens to you!

FAQ

What is unique about LeLaWalks?
LeLaWalks helps you to achieve two things at the same time: to learn a new language and to increase your physical activity. Ou\ r material is completely audio based and highly customisable to match your needs and learning speed.
Is audio based material effective?
We learn our first language by listening, so it is the most natural method. Studies also show that we learn best in connection to moderate physical activity. Portable devices make make it now easy and convenient to learn on the move.
What level of material will be available?
We will first offer beginner level material.
